# Day 25 – AI Shark Tank Simulator

## Objective

Build and test an AI-powered Shark Tank Simulator using Claude AI. The application evaluates startup ideas through simulated investor interactions, scoring systems, valuation analysis, and funding recommendations.

---

## Project Overview

The AI Shark Tank Simulator is a business evaluation web application that allows users to pitch startup ideas to virtual investors. The system simulates investor behavior, asks questions, evaluates responses, and generates funding decisions.

---

### Startup Information Collection

## Startup Idea Used

### Startup Name

EcoTrack AI

### Problem Statement

Many households struggle to monitor and reduce their environmental impact due to a lack of actionable sustainability insights.

### Solution

An AI-powered platform that tracks energy usage, waste generation, and carbon footprint while providing personalized recommendations.

### Revenue Model

Subscription-based model with premium analytics and enterprise sustainability reporting.

### Target Audience

Environmentally conscious consumers, homeowners, and businesses.

### Funding Ask

$250,000
