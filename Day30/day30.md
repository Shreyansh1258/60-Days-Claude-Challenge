# Day 30 – Supply Chain Optimization Simulator

## Overview

This project explores the fundamentals of supply chain optimization through an interactive enterprise simulation built with React. The simulator demonstrates how businesses improve existing supply chain systems by making strategic decisions rather than rebuilding operations from scratch. Users experience how choices related to suppliers, manufacturing, warehousing, transportation, and inventory influence overall business performance.

## Objectives

* Understand the core principles of supply chain optimization.
* Learn how sourcing, production, warehousing, transportation, and inventory work together.
* Explore business trade-offs between cost, delivery speed, operational risk, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
* Analyze the impact of strategic decisions using an interactive simulation.
* Compare different optimization strategies across multiple business scenarios.

## Features

* Interactive React-based enterprise simulation
* Randomly generated company profiles for varied scenarios
* Multiple supply chain strategy options
* Real-time business metric updates
* Overall Supply Chain Score calculation
* Performance dashboard with strengths and weaknesses
* Risk analysis and optimization recommendations
* Replay support for experimenting with different strategies

## Key Concepts Covered

* Supply Chain Optimization
* Supplier Selection
* Manufacturing Strategy
* Warehouse Management
* Transportation Planning
* Inventory Optimization
* Cost vs Speed Trade-offs
* Risk Management
* Sustainability Considerations
* Customer Satisfaction Metrics
* Continuous Process Improvement

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this project, you will be able to:

* Explain the importance of continuous improvement in supply chain management.
* Evaluate the effects of different sourcing and logistics strategies.
* Understand how inventory decisions influence operational efficiency.
* Balance competing business objectives while optimizing supply chain performance.
* Interpret business metrics to identify strengths, weaknesses, and improvement opportunities.
* Apply strategic thinking to optimize enterprise operations.

## Technologies Used

* React
* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript

## Conclusion

This project provides a practical introduction to supply chain optimization by combining business concepts with interactive decision-making. Through multiple simulation runs, users gain a deeper understanding of how interconnected supply chain components affect organizational performance and learn to make informed decisions that balance efficiency, cost, resilience, and customer satisfaction.
