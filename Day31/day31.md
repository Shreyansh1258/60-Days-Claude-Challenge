# Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower Simulation

## 📌 Objective

Build an interactive **AI Supply Chain Control Tower** simulation using Claude AI. The application demonstrates how enterprises monitor logistics, suppliers, inventory, and transportation while making real-time operational decisions that impact key business metrics.

---

## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

- Understand Supply Chain Control Tower fundamentals.
- Learn real-time operational decision making.
- Analyze the impact of decisions on business KPIs.
- Build an enterprise-grade dashboard using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Gain experience using AI to generate complete web applications.

---

## 💻 Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (Vanilla)
- Claude AI
---

## 📈 Key Features

- Real-time operational alerts
- Interactive decision-making simulation
- Dynamic KPI updates
- Enterprise-style dashboard
- Supply chain performance monitoring
- Multiple simultaneous incident handling

---

## 📊 Business KPIs

The simulation tracks:

- Customer Satisfaction
- Operational Costs
- Inventory Health
- Revenue
- Supplier Performance
- Delivery Efficiency

Every decision directly influences these KPIs, demonstrating the trade-offs involved in supply chain management.

---

## 📚 Key Learnings

- Supply Chain Control Towers improve operational visibility.
- Prioritizing high-impact incidents minimizes business disruption.
- Every operational decision affects multiple KPIs.
- Interactive dashboards provide actionable business insights.
- AI can rapidly generate realistic enterprise simulation applications.
